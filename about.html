✦ Our Story

Mid Michigan Drift Club

The Mid Michigan Drift Club is a community-driven motorsports club built for drivers and fans who share a passion for the art of drifting. Based in the heart of Michigan, we call Jackson Speedway our home track — one of the premier venues in the region for wheel-to-wheel action, tire smoke, and high-angle entries.

Whether you're strapping into a purpose-built drift car for the first time or you're a seasoned competitor chasing angle, we welcome everyone. Our events range from beginner clinics to full competition days, so there's always a way to get involved — no matter where you are in your drift journey.

We're more than just an event series. MMDC is a community. You'll find us wrenching in the pits, sharing tips in the Facebook group, and cheering each other on from the track wall. Come out, turn some laps, and lay down some rubber with us.
