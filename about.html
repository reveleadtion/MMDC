About MMDC

✦ Our Story

Built for the Love of the Slide

The Mid Michigan Drift Club is Michigan's most active grassroots drift community — and we run it the right way. No velvet ropes, no gatekeeping. Just real cars, real drivers, and real people standing at a fence watching something they've never seen this close before.

We run a full seven-event season at Jackson Speedway, just off Page Ave in Jackson, MI — easy to get to from Lansing, Ann Arbor, or anywhere in between. Whether you're coming to watch for the first time or you've been chasing angle for years, there's a place for you here.

✦ For Spectators ✦

You've Never Seen Anything Like This

Drifting is the loudest, smokiest, most sideways thing happening in Michigan on a weekend — and it's one of the best-value days out you'll find. Kids under 18 get in free, adults are just $15. Walk the paddock, get close to the cars, and watch drivers push the limits in ways that will genuinely surprise you.

  • 🎟️ Under 18 free · Adults $15 admission
  • 🅿️ Free parking at Jackson Speedway
  • 🔓 Open paddock — walk right up to the cars
  • 🍔 Rotating food vendors on site — or bring your own
  • 📸 Perfect for photos — action happens right in front of you

✦ First Time At A Drift Event?

Here's What to Expect

01

Show Up & Park

Free parking at Jackson Speedway. Adults are $15 at the gate — kids under 18 always get in free. No pre-registration needed for spectators, just show up.

02

Walk the Paddock

Unlike stadium motorsport, grassroots drifting has an open paddock. You can walk right up to the cars, talk to drivers, and see what makes a drift build tick.

03

Find Your Spot

The action at Jackson Speedway happens close. Find a spot at the fence near a corner and watch cars come in at full lock — loud, smoky, and completely controlled chaos.

04

Eat, Hang, Repeat

Rotating food vendors are on site at events — or pack your own. It's a full day out. Bring the crew, find a spot at the fence, and stay until the last run.
