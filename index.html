Michigan's Best Grassroots Drift Events — Free to Spectate

The Mid Michigan Drift Club runs the most active drift event series in the state of Michigan, with seven events at Jackson Speedway from March through November 2026. Whether you're a longtime car enthusiast or just looking for something wild to do on a weekend near Jackson, MI — spectator admission is free. No experience needed. Just show up, find a spot at the fence, and watch some of Michigan's best drift drivers slide sideways at full throttle.

Our events range from beginner-friendly drift clinics to full open competition days, making MMDC the perfect entry point for anyone curious about grassroots motorsport in Michigan. Jackson Speedway sits at 3363 Page Ave in Jackson, MI — easy to reach from Lansing, Ann Arbor, and the greater mid-Michigan area.
